New Romantics

New Romantics is a typeface inspired by the New Romantics era, a pop culture, punk rock-esque movement which emerged from the nightclub scene in London and Birmingham, UK in the early 1980s. In emulating the nature of the era, New Romantics possesses an edgy, abstract design often interrupted by angles and lines. With this in mind, New Romantics is for the average modernist with an admiration for edge, modern art, and the distinguishable presence of shape within typographic design. Although the New Romantics Era left just as quickly as it came, New Romantics will live on through the covers of smart phone applications, on the header of magazines, outside of arcades, as band names, on t-shirts, on smart phone cases, and in the hearts and minds of those who appreciate it's aesthetic appeal and historical significance.
